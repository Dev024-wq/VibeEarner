How to Join as an LIC Agent

1
Check Your Eligibility

Minimum age 18, 10th pass, Indian citizenship (as per Agents’ Regulations, 2017).

2
Complete Official Training

Attend LIC’s authorized pre-recruitment program.

3
Pass IRDAI Exam

Clear the IC-38 examination for agent licensing.

4
Interview and Onboarding

Attend LIC branch interview and begin your journey.
