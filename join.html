How to Join as an LIC Agent
1
Check Your Eligibility
Minimum age 18, 10th pass, and Indian citizenship. (As per Agents’ Regulations 2017)
2
Complete Official Training
Attend LIC's authorized pre-recruitment program (offline or online).
3
Pass IRDAI Exam
Clear the IC-38 examination to get licensing.
4
Branch Interview & Onboarding
Attend LIC branch interview and start your journey.
