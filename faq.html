Frequently Asked Questions

What are the eligibility criteria to become an LIC agent?

Minimum age of 18 years, minimum education qualification is 10th pass or equivalent, and Indian citizenship as per the Agents’ Regulations, 2017.

How much can I earn as an LIC agent?

Income depends on your efforts with commissions from new business premiums and renewal premiums. There is no fixed salary, but the earning potential is unlimited based on your dedication.

What training do I need to become an LIC agent?

LIC provides official pre-recruitment training. Additionally, you need to clear the IRDAI IC-38 examination to get licensed as an agent.

Can I work flexible hours as an LIC agent?

Yes! Being an LIC agent offers you the flexibility to manage your own work hours and grow your business at your own pace.

How do I apply to become an LIC agent?

You need to apply through the official LIC recruitment procedure, complete the training, pass the certification exam, and go through an interview at the LIC branch office. Our Join As Agent page provides detailed steps.
